About


Hi! I’m
Kirby Bilbao;

I’m a self-taught game developer and artist, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing at West Visayas State University. My goal? To make games that are not just fun, but also fun to make.

As a game developer, I specialize in creating PSX/PS1-style games, capturing that nostalgic, low-poly aesthetic with a mix of surreal, eerie, and immersive experiences. I enjoy blending retro visuals, atmospheric storytelling, and experimental gameplay to create something that feels both familiar and unsettling—like a half-remembered dream.

Beyond game development, I have experience in 3D modeling, animation, pixel art, and UI/UX design. I’m also proficient in Unity, Godot, Unreal Engine, and Blender, and I love diving into C#, Java, and Python to bring my ideas to life.

I’m always looking for new challenges, collaborations, and opportunities to create something unique. If you’re into weird, nostalgic, or just plain fun games, let’s connect!
